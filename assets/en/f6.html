Luxurious Livings in
Berlin-Lichtenberg

The 12 luxurious apartments and two villas for singles and families in modern, timeless architecture make the dream of owning a home come true. Floor-to-ceiling, double-glazed windows ensure rooms flooded with light. Well-designed floor plans offer space for different ways of living. A minimum of load-bearing interior walls makes individual floor plan design possible.
The apartments are equipped with high quality real wood parquet and individually adjustable underfloor heating. The bathrooms are tiled with high-quality natural stone and impress with modern, contemporary furnishings from well-known suppliers.
Carefree, generation-friendly mobility to the front door is ensured by a lift in the front apartment building.
Barrier-free living is possible in many apartments. With its private storage space, the basement enables safe storage and offers enough space for storing bicycles. Some residential units also have a practical utility room.
Each unit has a spacious terrace or balcony. On the property, green areas with a playground for children and private gardens offer an oasis inviting you to relax and linger.
